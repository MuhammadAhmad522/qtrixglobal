Technical Application Protocol

01

Surface Preparation

Substrate must be clean, dry, and free of sharp protrusions. For bitumen, apply primer to ensure maximum adhesion.

02

Lapping & Bonding

Maintain a minimum 150mm overlap. For Polythene, use double-sided jointing tape. For Bitumen, ensure a full torch-on melt fusion.

03

Protection Layer

Immediately protect membranes from backfilling or construction traffic using protective boards or sand blinding.
